What is RRK?

The Recursive‑Relational Framework (RRK) is a unified theory of system dynamics. It describes how systems evolve through the interplay of recursive feedback and relational coupling. RRK provides a conceptual, numerical, analytical, and global perspective on systemic change.

  • RRK 2.1 – Numerical dynamics and simulation

RRK 4.1 – Stability Spaces

Why some ensembles remain stable while others collapse

Abstract

RRK 4.1 describes the regions in which ensembles of recursive systems remain stable. Stability emerges from the structure of the couplings.

1. Stability Space

A stability space is a region in the state space in which the ensemble does not diverge.

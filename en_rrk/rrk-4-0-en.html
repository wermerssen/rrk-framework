RRK 4.0 – Meta‑Dynamics

How many recursive systems jointly generate global patterns

Abstract

RRK 4.0 describes the dynamics of many interacting recursive systems. Meta‑dynamics emerge from the coupling of individual recursions.

1. System Ensemble

An ensemble consists of many recursive systems:

\[ \{ S^{(1)}, S^{(2)}, \dots, S^{(n)} \} \]

2. Coupling

Systems influence one another:

\[ S^{(i)}_{t+1} = R(S^{(i)}_t) + C(S^{(j)}_t) \]

3. Meta‑Dynamics

The global dynamics arise from the sum of all local recursions.

4. Transition to RRK 4.1

RRK 4.1 investigates the stability spaces of such ensembles.

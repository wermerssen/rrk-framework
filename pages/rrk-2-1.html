RRK 2.1 – Numerical Dynamics

Simulation of recursive‑relational systems

Overview

RRK 2.1 develops the numerical layer of the recursive‑relational framework. It focuses on simulation methods for systems defined by recursive feedback and relational coupling.

Discrete‑Time Dynamics

Systems are represented as iterated maps with relational dependencies. Numerical exploration reveals attractors, cycles, and transient regimes.

Parameter Exploration

RRK 2.1 emphasizes systematic parameter scans to uncover qualitative changes in system behavior and to prepare the ground for RRK 3.0.

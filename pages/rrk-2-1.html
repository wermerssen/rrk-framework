RRK 2.1 – Numerical Dynamics Model

RRK 2.1 implements the conceptual theory of RRK 2.0 as a nonlinear numerical simulation model. It defines the system variables, coupling functions, drift terms, and saturation dynamics.

The model allows exploration of attractor formation, bifurcations, oscillatory regimes, and collapse scenarios through parameter sweeps and time‑series analysis.

RRK 2.2 – Stability & Bifurcation Theory

RRK 2.2 extends the numerical model of RRK 2.1 by deriving analytical stability conditions, fixed points, Jacobians, and bifurcation structures. It formalizes how systems transition between stable, oscillatory, and chaotic regimes.

The theory provides tools for identifying attractor boundaries, collapse thresholds, and transition manifolds across parameter spaces.

RRK 2.2 – Analytical Structure

Stability and relational structure

Overview

RRK 2.2 introduces the analytical layer of the framework. It studies stability, invariants, and structural properties of recursive‑relational systems.

Relational Stability

Stability is defined in terms of relational configurations rather than purely local eigenvalues, preparing the shift toward global perspectives in RRK 3.0.

Analytical Tools

RRK 2.2 collects analytical techniques that connect numerical observations from RRK 2.1 with the global geometry developed in RRK 3.0.
